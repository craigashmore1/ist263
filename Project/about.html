Our Story

We’ve always believed that beer is more than just a drink, it’s a culture, a craft, and a way of bringing people together. From the clinking of pint glasses to the laughter shared in neighborhood pubs, beer has a special way of creating memories. We wanted to capture that spirit and carry it into a place that most people overlook: the restroom.

That’s how the idea of the beer keg urinal was born. We realized there was something powerful about transforming an authentic stainless steel beer keg, a symbol of tradition, craftsmanship, and good times, into a functional piece of design that makes people smile every time they see it.

Our mission is simple: to blend the culture of beer with bold, functional design. Each keg urinal is carefully repurposed and finished by hand, ensuring it’s not only durable but also a true statement piece. Whether you run a busy brewery, a local pub, or just want to make your man cave legendary, our products bring personality and practicality together in one unforgettable package.

We’re proud to give these kegs a second life, one that celebrates beer in a new and unexpected way. For us, it’s not just about making urinals, it’s about creating conversation starters, unforgettable experiences, and a touch of humor that makes your venue stand out.
