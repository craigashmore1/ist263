Our Urinal Beer Kegs are crafted from 100% food-grade stainless steel, designed to wow your guests and turn any restroom into a conversation piece.

Perfect for bars, breweries, pubs, and man caves, each Urinal Beer Keg adds a bold, industrial flair while maintaining durability and functionality.

All Urinal Beer Kegs are fabricated in the U.S.A. using authentic stainless steel kegs. We offer multiple finish options, including:

  • Brushed stainless finish
  • Sandblasted fine pebble finish
  • Custom fabrication options (by request)

Urinal Beer Keg Includes:

  • 3/4" threaded fitting compatible with manual or sensor flush valves (flush valve NOT included)
  • Spray nozzle
  • 100% handcrafted construction
  • Mounting bracket for easy wall installation
  • Polished to a medium luster
  • Food-grade stainless steel keg for sanitary use
